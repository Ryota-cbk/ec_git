About

Suis(スイ)では生物学的な性差を尊重しながらも
身なりにおいて社会・文化的な性差を介することなく、
ひとりひとりの「自分らしさ」の表現に寄与する。

"Gender" means social and cultural differences about sex, while "sex" refers to
physical and biological differences "Suis" proposes a concept which
expression of each person's "personality" without going through
social and cultural gender differences in their appearance while respecting biological gender differences
